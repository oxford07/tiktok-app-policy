Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how this application ("the App") collects, uses, and protects information when you use it to connect your TikTok account and upload content.

1. Information We Collect

When you authorize the App via TikTok's OAuth login, the App may receive basic profile information (such as your display name and avatar) and an access token that allows the App to publish content to TikTok on your behalf. The App does not collect or store any information beyond what is necessary to perform video uploads.

2. How We Use Information

Information received from TikTok is used solely to authenticate your account and to publish the video content you choose to upload. We do not sell, rent, or share this information with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Access tokens and related credentials are stored securely and only for as long as necessary to operate the App. You may revoke the App's access at any time through your TikTok account settings.

4. Third-Party Services

The App interacts with TikTok's API to perform uploads. TikTok's own Privacy Policy governs how TikTok handles your data on its platform.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

You may request deletion of any data associated with your account by contacting the developer. Revoking app permissions in your TikTok account settings will also remove the App's access to your account.

6. Children's Privacy

This App is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect information from individuals under the age required by TikTok's Terms of Service.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised Policy.

8. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer through the contact information provided on the TikTok Developer Portal app listing.
